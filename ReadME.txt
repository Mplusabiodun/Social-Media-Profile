Social Media Profile 

A social media profile is an individual's personal page or account on a social media platform, 
which is used to connect and interact with others online. These profiles often include a range 
of personal details, such as the user's name, photo, location, and interests. 

The specific features and layout of a social media profile can vary significantly depending on 
the platform, such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc. Social media profiles play a 
significant role in digital identity and online socialization, and they are also used for 
professional networking and personal branding.
